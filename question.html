[Announcement 2023.06.21]

Hello colleagues, we would like to inform you about some updates regarding the Task 2 dataset. The revised version, colab_train.csv, has been modified to adjust the time span of the training dataset to a more realistic setting. The new time range is 2020.2-2021.8 instead of 2020.2-2022.2. Additionally, the corresponding edge list colab_train_edge_list.csv has been updated accordingly.

Please make sure to utilize the training dataset with the corrected time span when training your model. Furthermore, when submitting your paper, please include your code.

Please note that these changes may affect the leaderboard. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and appreciate your cooperation. Thank you for your understanding.
