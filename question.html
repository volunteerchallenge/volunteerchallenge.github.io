[Announcement 2023.07.15]
1. Challenge Winner and Paper Selection Rules:
- Winners will be selected according to the best performance for each single task, separately. Therefore, one winner for each task will be selected.
- Up to four papers will be accepted to be presented at the conference.
- Papers will be peer reviewed as a regular conference paper. They will be scored based on their model performance on the private test data (75%) and paper quality (25%). Paper quality metrics include clarity of method, correctness, reproducibility and originality (bonus).
2. Paper Summary Submission:
- Submit a one-page summary about your methodology and numerical results by July 20th.
- Please follow the paper submission instructions on MLSP website. Make sure to choose the track “MLSP2023 - Data Competition Papers” on CMT.
- If you have already finished writing the paper, you can use it instead of the summary.
- You must submit the initial summary to be eligible for the final paper submission.
3. Final Paper Submission:
- You can upload the full version of your paper to the paper submission system before the final submission deadline.
- The new deadline is July 27th.
