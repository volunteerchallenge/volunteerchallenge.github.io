[Announcement 2023.06.21]

Hello colleagues, we we made some important updates regarding the Task 2 dataset. The affected files, colab_train.csv and colab_train_edge_list.csv, have been modified to adjust the training data time span to a more realistic setting. The new time range is 2020.2-2021.8 instead of 2020.2-2022.2.

Please make sure to use the training dataset with the corrected time span when training your model. Furthermore, when submitting your paper, please include your code.

Please note that these changes may affect the leaderboard. We sincerely apologize for the inconvenience this may cause and appreciate your cooperation.
